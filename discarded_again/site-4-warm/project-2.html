AI-powered Amazon Seller Support

Unified Support experience and connecting with Customer Support through Selling Assistant

Role Lead UX Designer
Tools Figma, Builder.io
Team 2 UX designers, 1 UX researcher, 3 product managers, 2 developers
Timeline January - September 2025
Project Hero Screenshot

Impact

10M Issue creation elimination
$50M Cost savings
-50% Negative Response Rate

Overview

Designed a unified support experience that combines AI-powered self-service with seamless escalation to human support when needed.

Scenario 1: Issue resolved through self-service

Sellers interact with an AI assistant that understands their context, pulls relevant account data, and provides personalized solutions without needing to create a support ticket.

Self-service Flow Screenshot
  1. Seller describes issue in natural language
  2. AI identifies issue type and pulls relevant data
  3. Personalized solution presented with step-by-step guidance
  4. Seller confirms resolution
Self-service UI Screenshot

Scenario 2: Issue resolved through human support

When AI cannot resolve the issue, it seamlessly transitions to human support with full context, eliminating the need for sellers to repeat information.

Human Support Flow Screenshot
  1. AI determines issue requires human intervention
  2. Full context and attempted solutions passed to associate
  3. Associate resolves issue with complete background
  4. Resolution logged for AI learning
Human Support UI Screenshot

Usability Testing

10 sessions conducted with sellers and associates

Key Findings

  • Sellers appreciated not having to repeat information
  • Trust in AI increased when it acknowledged limitations
  • Clear escalation path reduced frustration
